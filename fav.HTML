APJ ABDUL KALAM

Description

Abdul Kalam, also known as A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, was a renowned Indian scientist, engineer, and statesman. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he played a crucial role in India's missile development program. Kalam served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007, earning the nickname "People's President" for his simplicity, humility, and connection with the common people.
